I was born in the city of Smolensk, Russia in 1978. Graduated from the high school with honors in 1996. Graduated Summa Cum Laude from the Department of Computer Aided Design, Manufacturing and Engineering, School of Information Sciences, at Ivanovo State Power University, Russia in 2001. Defended Candidate of Technical Sciences dissertation "Models and methods of advanced online training operating personnel of Computer Aided Design systems" at Ivanovo State Power University, Russia 2004 (advisor Evgeny R. Panteleev). Defended Doctor of Philosophy thesis "Providing Service-Based Personalization in an Adaptive Hypermedia System" at the School of Information Sciences, University of Pittsburgh in 2010 (advisor Peter L. Brusilovsky). From 2010 till 2013 - a Post Doctoral Fellow at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University where I worked with Kenneth R. Koedinger and Geoffrey J. Gordon. From 2013 to 2016, I worked as a Research Scientist at Carnegie Learning, Inc. Since early 2016, I'm back at theHuman-Computer Interaction Institute, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University as a Project Scientist, where I work with Kenneth R. Koedinger.
